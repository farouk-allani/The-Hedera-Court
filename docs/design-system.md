# Design System

The Hedera Court uses an old English law library style: cream paper, oxblood ink, brass dividers, moss accents, and worn docket textures.

## Tokens

- `--cream: #f4ecd8`
- `--oxblood: #6b1c1c`
- `--ink: #1c1614`
- `--brass: #8a6d3b`
- `--moss: #4a5a3a`
- `--shadow: #0e0a08`

## Typography

- Display and body: Crimson Pro and Cormorant Garamond
- Plaque labels: Cormorant SC
- Verdict and receipt text: IBM Plex Mono

## Motion

- Hero papers drift subtly over the illustrated courtroom scene.
- Filing and defense desks use evidence art headers and animated paper slips.
- Verdict text uses a 34ms typewriter cadence.
- Stamps drop from scale 1.4 to 1.0 with slight rotation.
- NFT cards slide upward and settle flat.
